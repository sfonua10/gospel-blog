CHARITY Charity sufferth long What does it mean to be Long-Suffering? suffer verb 1. experience or be subjected to (something bad or unpleasant) 2. allow (someone) to do something long-suffering adj having or showing patience in spite of troubles, especially those caused by other people Examples in scriptures Jesus Christ Nephi Alma and Ammon My definition of patience: Going through a bad, unpleasant, humiliating experience and not getting angry, irritated, upset, anxious or frustrated.
